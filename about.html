Our Story

Founded in 1996 in Carbondale, Colorado, Dirt Bikes USA was built by riders for riders. Our bikes combine world-class components and engineering with the passion and feedback of real racers.

We take pride in building off-road motorcycles that dominate races and deliver an unbeatable ride across any terrain. Our team includes experienced engineers, designers, and racing enthusiasts.
